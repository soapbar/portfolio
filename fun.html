'Confessions of a KDramaholic' Blog

A blog I decided to start my freshman year in high school, just documenting my life after watching my first Korean drama, Boys over Flowers. It has since been a place where I rant, celebrate, and just let out my feelings. I keep it as a record of my journey and my thought process while watching various Korean dramas.

Read it on WordPress.


Sophie YouTube Videos

Because I always was thankful for the subtitles that others had online when I didn't know much of the langugage, I decided to make a YouTube channel that I could upload short videos with my translations. I started uploading lyric videos that I couldn't find sufficient translations online. I enjoy making short videos for friends and for myself, which can be mainly found in my playlist section here.
